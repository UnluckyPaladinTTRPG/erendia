Summary

The party came together at the Journey's End tavern in the city of Leftwyne. After a short time in the tavern, Gideon took down a notice of a missing person called Grace Smith. The group, composed of Wynivyre, Gideon, Skritcha, and Myam-ix headed to Lisanna's Lounge, where they learned about a man by the name of Dr. Ramsey who supposedly interacted with Grace before her disappearance.

Shortly after leaving Lisanna's Lounge, the party witnessed an explosion that destroyed the upper floor of a house in Northtown and, after brief deliberation, decided to enter and search for survivors. In the house they discovered a dead chemist and a note that mentioned Dr. Ramsey and gave the rough location of Warehouse Row.

The party was confronted by the Northtown guards and their captain, who grew suspicious of their involvement in the explosion. After some back and forth between the party and the guard captain, they were allowed to continue their investigation and thus headed to the Warehouse Row.

Upon discovering, and entering, the warehouse in question, the party found that the warehouse was pretty much abandoned on a first glance. In the foreman's office, Gideon found a secret compartment in a desk that contained designs for a scientific process that involved operation on human bodies.

The party then discovered a secret lab in the rear of the warehouse and were attacked by two warehouse workers that acted strange and zombie-like. Just as they entered the lab, a scientist that they presumed to be Dr. Ramsey used a teleportation circle to make a hasty escape.

After dispatching the warehouse workers, the Northtown guard arrested the party for their presence at the scene of two additional apparent murders shortly after their involvement in the murder of the chemist in the hour prior.

The guards took the party into custody, detaining them in the Northtown jail, where the events of Session 1 came to a close.
