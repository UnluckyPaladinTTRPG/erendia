First Age

Year 0

The storms and earthquakes finally cease, and the remnants of the mortal civilizations begin anew in Erendia. Contemporary history begins here, as much was lost in the great cataclysm of the Shattering.

Year 229

Opening of the World Gate. The ancient mages manage to tear open a rift that will be a pathway between realities. The Gate was never used, for passing was too dangerous for the airships of that time.

Year 230

The World Gate Collapses. The pathway to other realities becomes unstable due to the actions of Ardonax Spellweaver, the archmage of Tarnon. This causes the rift to collapse into itself and create a massive chasm known as Spellweaver's Curse.

Year 241

The Rot War begins. Abyssal creatures begin pouring from Spellweaver's Curse and invading Erendia. The Verdant Sea is lost to the corruption spewing forth from the newly named Voidrift.

Year 368

Formation of the Alliance of All People. The mortal races of Erendia come together to form an army greater than any before it in order to protect Erendia and drive back the abyssal monsters.

Year 392

The turn of the tide. The Alliance of All People discovers a new weapon against the abyssal creatures and use it to begin advancing into the corrupted lands around the Voidrift.

Year 411

The Verdant Sea is reclaimed and the corruption slowly expunged from its soil. The Alliance of All People advances on the Voidrift.

Year 413

Rot War finally comes to an end. With the Battle of the Voidrift, the Alliance of All People manages to drive back the abyss and seal the Great Rift. This ushers in a new era of prosperity across Erendia as the last agents of the abyssal invasion are hunted down and exterminated.

Second Age

Year 0

The settling of Erendia. With the Voidrift finally sealed, the people that survived the Rot War begin to expand across unexplored territories of Erendia.

Year 48

The founding of the Starlight Basin. The dwarves discover a place deep in the mountains that they flock to and build a giant city.

Year 112

The elves disperse to the west of Erendia and settle the massive forests there.

Year 129 

The elven kingdom of Kelbruine is founded.

Year 639

The southern coast is settled and the kingdom of Baharat is founded.

Year 580

Settlers land on the Silver Coast and construct the city of Leftwyne.

Year 682

The kingdoms of Tir Leonas and Greystone are founded.

Year 717

The Bay Alliance is formed and restoration begins on the city of Leftwyne on the Silver Coast.
